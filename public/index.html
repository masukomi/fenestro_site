Fenestro Screenshots Usually screenshots are a great way to show off your app, but I’ll display whatever you tell me to, just like your favorite web browser does. Thus, my screenshots could show anything. If you ask Fenestro to render just one piece of HTML you’ll see a simple window with your HTML rendered in it. If you ask Fenestro to a bunch of files quickly, he’ll group them and give you a nav bar to change between them.
